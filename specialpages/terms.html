marmalade-repo will host your Emacs package and provide an Emacs ELPA archive compatible index of it so long as:

  • your package is licenced under the GPL or a GPL compatible licence
  • your package is free from malicious effects
  • your package conforms to the ELPA standards for defining packages

No warranty is offered for marmalade-repo. It may be up or down. It may be broken or working.

I endeavour to keep it working. But it's not always possible. Thanks!
